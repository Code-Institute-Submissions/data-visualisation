What chart's are representing?
On Variability page:

Charts are created in such a way that periods where variability is higher than average are colored on yellow. You can simply see that abnormal spike in price:

  • Started in October 2017
  • Exploded January 2018
  • Normalized in July 2018
You can analyse this by yourself. Also if you will look and compare when this phenomenon happen. It looks like it started firstly on Bitcoin in November, then after on Etherum half December and last one was Litecoin in February 2018
